    About the project

    Nestled on the prestigious Crescent side of Palm Jumeirah, Passo by Beyond sets a new benchmark for premium residential living in Dubai. This exclusive development features 625 meticulously crafted units across two G+14 towers and elegant G+2 villas, each offering unparalleled comfort and sophistication.


    From cozy 1-bedroom apartments to expansive 6-bedroom homes, every residence is thoughtfully designed to meet diverse lifestyle needs while embracing natural light, refined finishes, and seamless indoor-outdoor living. With completion slated for December 2029, Passo provides a rare opportunity to secure a prestigious waterfront address in one of the world’s most sought-after destinations.


    Prime Location: Palm Jumeirah’s Crescent


    Positioned on the coveted Crescent, Passo offers residents direct access to pristine beaches and uninterrupted views of the Arabian Gulf. This location combines the tranquility of private island living with easy reach to Dubai’s vibrant urban hubs.


    Location Highlights:


    • Exclusive Crescent Position: Private beaches with panoramic Gulf views
    • Marina Access: Close to top-tier marinas and water sports facilities
    • Luxury Hotel Proximity: Walking distance to renowned resorts and five-star hospitality
    • Fine Dining and Cafés: Surrounded by gourmet restaurants and sophisticated cafés
    • Privacy and Prestige: Secluded island living with maximum exclusivity


    Morning strolls along the sand can effortlessly transition into evenings spent enjoying Dubai’s world-class dining and entertainment, all within the secure Palm community.


    Architectural and Interior Design


    Passo by Beyond combines contemporary coastal aesthetics with flowing, organic forms inspired by the ocean. The development emphasizes curvilinear architecture, expansive balconies, and premium materials to create residences that feel open, serene, and sophisticated.


    Design Highlights:


    • Curvilinear Architecture: Mirroring the movement of ocean waves
    • Elevated Podiums: Ensuring optimal views and privacy
    • Integrated Water Features: Enhancing serenity and ambiance
    • Generous Balconies: Ideal for entertaining or unwinding outdoors
    • Premium Materials: High-end finishes throughout interiors
    • Rooftop Spaces: Offering panoramic vistas and exclusive leisure areas


    The design seamlessly integrates smart home technologies and sustainable principles, balancing luxury with environmental responsibility.


    Amenities and Facilities


    Passo delivers a full suite of premium amenities, designed to enhance wellness, leisure, and daily convenience.


    Recreational and Wellness Facilities:


    • Direct private beach access with pristine sands
    • Cutting-edge fitness centers with advanced equipment
    • Dedicated wellness and spa facilities
    • Infinity-edge swimming pools overlooking the Gulf
    • Landscaped gardens and parks for peaceful retreats


    Exclusive Services:

    • 24/7 concierge and security services
    • Valet parking and professional property management
    • Private dining and entertainment spaces


    Lifestyle and Community


    Living at Passo blends privacy with community, offering spaces where families and individuals can relax, socialize, and thrive.


    Community Features:


    • Family-Oriented Environment: Safe and secure spaces for all ages
    • Social Spaces: Common areas that encourage interaction
    • International Community: Reflecting Dubai’s cosmopolitan character
    • Exclusive Resident Events: Seasonal gatherings and celebrations
    • Maintenance-Free Living: Comprehensive property management for peace of mind


    Residents enjoy a balance of sophisticated urban lifestyle with the calm of waterfront living, creating an environment that supports both relaxation and connection.


    Investment Potential


    With starting prices from AED 5.5 million, Passo by Beyond represents a strong investment opportunity. Its limited inventory, prime Crescent location, and premium specifications position it for significant capital appreciation and rental demand.


    Investment Highlights:


    • High-Value Location: Palm Jumeirah properties consistently outperform the wider market
    • Limited Supply: Ensures exclusivity and scarcity value
    • Rental Potential: Strong demand for luxury waterfront rentals
    • Completion by December 2029 with flexible payment plans
    • Premium Specifications: Smart, sustainable design with top-tier finishes


    Connectivity


    Despite its private island setting, Passo offers seamless access to Dubai’s key destinations.


    Transport Links:

    • 10-minute drive to Dubai Marina and entertainment hubs
    • Easy reach to DIFC and financial districts

    Passo by Beyond masterplan

    Passo by Beyond masterplan is an exclusive waterfront development by Beyond, designed as a part of Palm Jumeirah masterplan, located in the prestigious Palm Jumeirah masterplane. The project features two iconic G+14 towers alongside luxury villas, thoughtfully crafted to deliver a lifestyle of refined beachfront living. With 625 modern residences, Passo combines coastal-inspired architecture, elevated podiums, and panoramic Arabian Gulf views with premium finishes and serene water features. This development redefines contemporary living, offering expansive balconies, direct beach access, landscaped parks, and wellness-focused amenities, while also catering to maritime enthusiasts with marina access. Its prime location ensures seamless connectivity to Sheikh Zayed Road, the Trunk, and Fronds of Palm Jumeirah, complemented by proximity to world-class hotels, resorts, restaurants, and retail destinations.
